Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 13, 2026

Welcome to Triplens. Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Android application.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in using Google Authentication, we collect basic profile information such as your name and email address to create and manage your account.
  • Device Information: We may collect non-identifiable device information (like OS version) to ensure compatibility and improve application performance.

2. Use of Accessibility Services

Triplens utilizes the Android AccessibilityService API. This is a core feature required for the application to function.

  • Why we use it: The Accessibility Service is used strictly to read the screen content of specific ride-hailing applications to detect incoming ride requests and automate the "Accept" button click for driver safety and convenience.
  • Data Privacy: The text and screen content read by the Accessibility Service is processed locally on your device. We do not record, log, store, or transmit your screen data to any external servers.

3. Payment Processing

We use Cashfree Payments as our secure payment gateway for handling premium subscriptions. When you make a payment, your billing information is processed directly by Cashfree. We do not store your credit card or banking details on our servers.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including Firebase Authentication, to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app relies on third-party services that have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services & Firebase (Authentication)
  • Cashfree Payments (Payment Processing)

6. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify users of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact our support team.
