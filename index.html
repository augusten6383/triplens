Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 12, 2026

At Triplens, operated by Cube Solutions, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle any information you provide while using our application and website.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect minimal information required to verify subscription entitlements:

  • Account Info: Email address and name provided through Google Sign-In.
  • Service Data: Accessibility configurations, local pricing/distance filters stored locally on your device.

2. Accessibility Service (Crucial Disclosures)

Triplens utilizes the Android AccessibilityService API to scan dispatch dialogs and perform coordinates-based click inputs. This service is solely used to automate touch gestures for ride acceptance. No keystrokes, personal credentials, or other sensitive on-screen inputs are read, shared, or uploaded to any server.

3. Data Security

All subscription credentials and transaction summaries are stored in encrypted databases (Turso Cloud Integration) using secure authentication tokens to prevent unauthorized data access.
